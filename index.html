Healing

Healing superpower refers to the ability of a character to heal themselves or others. This superpower may include some component of empathy. Regeneration may also include regrowth, allowing the character to heal the natural world. A character with healing factor will bounce back from severe injuries that other beings can't, often with no scars or medical treatment.

Teleporting

Teleportation is a superpower that allows you to instantly travel to any location, travel through solid objects, and travel to other dimensions. It is also a very energy-efficient form of travel, as it requires no fuel and produces no pollution. Teleportation can accomplish most perks from other popular superpowers, such as flying and invisibility

Mind reading

Mind reading, also known as telepathy, is a supernatural power that allows people to access the thoughts and feelings of others without them having to say anything. Characters with this ability can read another person's thoughts and communicate with them mentally.
